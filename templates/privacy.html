Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate summaries and breakdowns, and maintain your account.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process such requests within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal third-party infrastructure (such as hosting providers) to operate the service, but these providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
